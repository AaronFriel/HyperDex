.. glossary::

   hyperspace
      A multidimensional coordinate system.

   hyperspace hashing
      A new hashing technique employed by HyperDex to store data in
      multi-dimensional space across multiple computers.

   space
      A space is the basic unit of organization within HyperDex.

   subspace
      A subset of the attributes of a space that are likely to be used together
      in a search predicate.
